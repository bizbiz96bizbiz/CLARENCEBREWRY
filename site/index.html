Our Heritage

From Bulldog Beer
to Your Weekend Away

"Authorities in 1885 lauded the beer's quality, as it garnered significant demand from hotelkeepers in the Clarence and Richmond regions, with substantial quantities being shipped to Sydney."

The Clarence Brewery produced legendary local beers — Red Crab Stout, Bulldog Beer — before closing in 1902. Restored in the 1970s and recognised by the National Trust, it now welcomes guests as intimate heritage accommodation.
