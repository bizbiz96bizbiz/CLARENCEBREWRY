Check-in is from 3:00 PM. Check-out is by 10:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out may be available on request — please enquire in advance at stay@theoldclarencebrewery.com.

Cancellation conditions are set by BayBnB at the time of booking and will be confirmed in your booking confirmation. We encourage guests to review the cancellation policy before completing their reservation.

Guests are responsible for the property during their stay. The Old Clarence Brewery is a heritage-listed building — we ask that all guests treat the space with care and respect. Damage to the property or its heritage fabric may result in additional charges.

The Old Clarence Brewery is a strictly non-smoking property, including all interior spaces and enclosed verandahs. Guests who wish to smoke must do so away from the building.
